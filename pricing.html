Questions

Good to know.

Do I need to bring anything?

Bring a water bottle and comfortable workout clothes. Cycling shoes are available to hire, and towels are provided. If you have your own SPD or Delta cleats, you're welcome to bring your own shoes.

What if I've never cycled indoors before?

Perfect — our coaches will walk you through bike setup, clip-in technique, and pacing before your first class. We suggest arriving 10 minutes early so there's no rush.

Can I pause or cancel my membership?

Unlimited memberships require a minimum 3-month commitment. After that, you can cancel with 30 days' notice. Pause requests (e.g. for travel or injury) are handled case by case — just reach out.

Is my first class really free?

Yes — new members can claim one complimentary class with no strings attached. Just book via the booking page and let us know it's your first time.
