Before You Arrive

What to expect.

🕐

Arrive Early

First time? Aim to arrive 10 minutes before class. Your coach will get you set up on the bike and answer any questions.

👟

Shoes & Kit

Hire cycling shoes from us or bring your own with SPD or Delta cleats. Towels are provided — just bring water and comfortable kit.

📵

Phones Away

We ask that phones stay out of the studio during class. It's 45 minutes just for you — you deserve the full focus.
