Rebecca

PT & Nutrition Specialist

Rebecca brings a holistic approach to personal training — one that looks at the whole picture, not just the workouts. Combining smart strength programming with practical, sustainable nutrition advice, she helps clients build bodies and habits that last.

Rebecca works with clients at all levels, with a particular focus on helping people navigate body composition changes in a healthy, positive way.

Rebecca's Approach

Whole-person
coaching.

Rebecca's coaching philosophy centres on sustainability. She doesn't believe in extreme approaches — instead, she helps clients build consistent habits around training and nutrition that create lasting change without burnout or obsession.

Her sessions balance challenge with recovery, and her nutrition advice is practical, flexible, and grounded in real life — not just what works in theory.
