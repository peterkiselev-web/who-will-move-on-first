Georgie

Cycling & Conditioning Specialist

Georgie leads cykl's group cycling programme with infectious energy and deeply considered session design. Her classes are known for their musical precision, motivating atmosphere, and the way they leave you wanting to come back for more.

Outside of the studio, Georgie specialises in conditioning and movement quality — helping clients build the physical foundations that support everything else they do.

Georgie's Approach

Energy that
carries you further.

Georgie's classes are interval-based and music-driven — but what sets them apart is the attention to structure. Every sprint, every recovery, every climb has a purpose. You leave having worked exactly as hard as you should have, and you always know why.

Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or stepping into a studio for the first time, Georgie meets you where you are and takes you somewhere better.
