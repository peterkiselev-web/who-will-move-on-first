Our Story

More than

Who We Are

Built for people
who mean it.

cykl was born out of a simple belief: that everyone deserves access to world-class coaching in an environment that actually feels good to be in. Not a faceless gym. Not a conveyor-belt class. A place where your coach knows your name and your goals.

We opened our doors in Chiswick with a small team, a tight-knit community, and an obsession with the details — the quality of the coaching, the music, the lighting, the programming. Everything is considered. Everything is intentional.

What Drives Us

Our values.

Three principles that guide everything we do — from how we programme sessions to how we welcome new members.

01

Community First

The energy in the room is as powerful as any training plan. We build genuine connections between members and coaches because we know that accountability and belonging are the greatest drivers of long-term change.

02

Expert Coaching

Every member of our team is fully qualified and continuously learning. We invest in our coaches so they can invest in you. Expect evidence-based programming, clear cues, and genuine expertise in every class.

03

Real Results

We're not here to tire you out — we're here to make you better. Every session has purpose. Every programme has progression. Whether your goal is fat loss, performance, or simply feeling good, we'll get you there.

The Space

Designed around
performance.

Our studio in Chiswick is purpose-built for the work. Premium bikes, calibrated acoustics, professional lighting rigs, and a layout that maximises energy while keeping everyone comfortable.

Whether it's your first class or your five hundredth, you'll walk in and feel like you belong here. That's by design.
